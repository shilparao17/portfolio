A New Tomorrow




September 2021 - June 2022


A New Tomorrow, 2022
Clay, glaze, faux vegetation, acrylic paint
102" x 57.67"


Explored the concept of connecting my [current] and future selves by depicting my dream career of combining my favourite subjects - engineering, technology, sustainability, and design - to create innovative buildings which are sustainable in all regards.

Portrayed the concept using two personally preferred art media studied in the Arts Canterbury Visual Arts Program: painting and ceramics.

A dark blue background is overlaid with engineering symbols and formulas, coupled with a white ceramic cityscape containing numerous windows and vegetation growing from balconies and rooftop gardens.

Took inspirations from the works of David Moreno and John Brickles.
