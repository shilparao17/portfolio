Solar Decathlon Competition




October 2022 - April 2023


Retro-fitted an existing house into a net-zero house as an architect for Warrior Home, a design team at the University of Waterloo.

Contributed to concept generation, napkin sketches, and SolidWorks and Revit designs.

Prioritized sustainability, affordability, and energy efficiency.

Partnered with the Kitchener-Waterloo Urban Native Wigwam Project to design the house as a culturally safe and affordable housing option for the Indigenous community in the Waterloo Region.

The design was pitched at the international Solar Decathlon competition and won first place for engineering.
