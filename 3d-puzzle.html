3D Printed Puzzle




September 2022 - Decemeber 2022


Worked in a team of five students to design and print a 12-piece 3D puzzle with a theme of the Faculty of Engineering at the University of Waterloo using SolidWorks.

Researched engineering and University of Waterloo symbols to develop a concept for the puzzle.

Drew an outline and divided the design into smaller components to fit like a puzzle together.

Used SolidWorks to develop and assemble the components, then printed the components.

Produced a video to show the assembly process of the puzzle.
